               CLIENT COMMUNICATION RULES
• Reply to all client messages within 4 business hours.
• Never go more than 5 days without a project update — even if there's nothing new. • Use Loom for complex explanations. It saves time and impresses clients.
• Document everything in writing. Scope creep kills small agency profitability.
• Always upsell with context: 'Based on what you told me, I think X would help...'

   THE 1% DAILY IMPROVEMENT RULE
You cannot build your SaaS and article platform in big bursts. The rule is simple: ship one small improvement every working day. That is 250 improvements per year. Compounded, this creates a competitive product.
• SaaS: Fix 1 bug OR ship 1 small feature OR write 1 unit test. Every. Single. Day. • Article platform: Draft 1 article outline, edit 1 draft, or publish 1 piece daily.
• Client sites: Maintain a handover checklist. Never deliver without documentation. • Keep a product roadmap in Notion. Prioritise by: user impact × effort.
